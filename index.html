Gunpla Showcase

This is a model I've custom painted."Gunpla" is short for Gundam plastic model. The model kit is MG Gundam Dynames by Bandai. I used Vallejo model air paint, and I've added an LED in the chest, metal parts to accent details and kit bashed(custom put together pieces from different model kits) to make the beam effect.
